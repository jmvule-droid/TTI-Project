Who We Are

Two horizons, one regional hub.

Together for Transformation Initiatives (TTI) targets structural community challenges at the intersection of climate, environment, and financial inclusion. Every local spoke we establish is designed to pay off immediately for the person running it while compounding systemic benefits across the surrounding community.

Short Term Focus

Technical Entrepreneurship

We supply marginalized youth and women with specialized skills, raw inputs, and structural technology so they can launch their own income-generating bio-ventures.

Long Term Vision

Systemic Independence

By cycling structural bio-waste back into farming networks, we rebuild degraded soil profiles and insulate local communities from costly global synthetic imports.

What We Do

Three operational pillars, one unified loop.

We avoid separate siloed agendas. Every integrated community hub we clear and deploy targets environmental, financial, and nutritional vulnerabilities in a single move.

Climate Action

Redirecting massive organic waste pipelines from open, hazardous urban landfills mitigates harmful methane emission peaks while producing soil-enriching frass.

Waste Diverted · Soil Restored

Economic Inclusion

Graduating students and community members operate as self-sustaining stakeholders of localized operations rather than insecure daily labor.

Spoke Co-Ownership · Skilled Income

Public Health

Clearing biological waste accumulations starves pathogenic insect vectors, while high-protein outputs strengthen livestock feed chains and local nutrition security.

Vector Control · Food Security
Our Operations

From bin to soil, in five tactical moves.

The Black Soldier Fly larva serves as our core bioconversion engine—cycling raw urban waste streams back into value-generating agricultural essentials.

1. Collection

Spokes harvest raw market, household, and industrial waste streams.

→

2. BSF Processing

Controlled insect colonies upcycle biological masses with precision tracking.

→

3. Protein Feed

Harvested larvae are transformed into balanced, protein-rich animal feeds.

→

4. Organic Frass

Nutrient-dense insect castings are refined into premium organic soil inputs.

→

5. Value Retention

Spoke co-owners sell value outputs back to regional farming communities.
